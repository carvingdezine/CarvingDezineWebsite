About the Studio

CARVING DEZINE is a boutique design studio founded by Tanvi, a designer with nearly two decades of experience crafting thoughtful digital and brand experiences.

Built on one simple belief: great design should stop you in your tracks. Not because it looks good, but because it feels right.

Over the years, Tanvi has worked with startups, scale-ups, and established organizations to design products, brands, and presentations that communicate clearly and connect with people. From Mobcast to ICICI Bank, UFO Movies to LifeLine Hospital, every project receives the same level of careful thinking, detail, and design craft.

CARVING DEZINE is intentionally small so every project gets the attention it deserves.
