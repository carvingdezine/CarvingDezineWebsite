About the Studio

Carving Dezine is a boutique design studio founded by Tanvi Shah — a designer with nearly two decades of experience crafting thoughtful digital and brand experiences.

Built on one simple belief: great design should stop you in your tracks. Not because it looks good — but because it feels right.

Over the years Tanvi has partnered with startups, scale-ups and established organisations across India and globally. From Mobcast to ICICI Bank, UFO Movies to LifeLine Hospital — every project gets the same obsessive attention.

Intentionally small. So every project gets the attention it deserves.
